About Me

Cali born. Texas raised. Mexican heart.
Amateur photographer. Web Developer.
Catholic Missionary from 2010-2020.
Catholic Priest from March 6, 2020 to December 24,2020.
On December 23rd, 2020 I suffered a mental, emotional and spiritual meltdown/crisis that threw me into a severe depression. I decided to start this blog as a form of therapy and to share my personal experience. I hope my story helps others have compassion towards their loved ones who may be going through a similar experience. And for those in the battle for their mental health: You are not alone. You deserve to be loved and helped.
